### To install GUI for your local MongoDB

Visit [MongoDB Compass](https://downloads.mongodb.com/compass/mongodb-compass-1.49.4-darwin-arm64.dmg)

## Connect to local database
1. Open the `Compass` application
2. Click on the '+' icon on the left hand-side beside the `CONNECTIONS` tab
3. Provide the following into the fields:
    - URI: mongodb://localhost:27107/
    - Name: acebook (or any name you would like)
4. Click 'Save and Connect'
